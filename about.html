About Aulo Bros

Worcester-based, family-owned, and committed to responsible waste management across Massachusetts and the New England region.

A Local Partner You Can Trust

Aulo Bros is a locally owned and operated waste and hauling company headquartered in Worcester, Massachusetts. We serve contractors, manufacturers, property managers, and municipalities with dependable roll-off services, hauling, demolition support, and customized waste programs.

Our team brings hands-on field experience to every project. We understand tight schedules, regulatory requirements, and cost pressures—and we structure our services to meet those demands without compromising performance or accountability.

From construction debris and concrete to organics and scrap metal, we design efficient, cost-effective solutions that keep projects moving while supporting long-term environmental responsibility.

Our Recycling-First Philosophy

Aulo Bros operates with a clear objective: recover, recycle, and repurpose as close to 100% of material as possible. Landfill disposal is treated as a last resort, not a default.

Construction and demolition debris
Concrete, asphalt, brick, and masonry
Clean wood and pallets
Scrap metal
Organics and food waste
Mixed recyclables

Our organic recovery programs include diverting food waste for conversion into animal feed, supporting agricultural reuse while reducing methane emissions and landfill volume. This closed-loop approach helps clients meet sustainability goals while remaining operationally efficient.
