Heavy Hauling in Worcester, MA

Specialized trucking for concrete, demolition debris, heavy equipment, and bulk materials throughout Central Massachusetts.

Moving the Heavy Loads Others Can't

Some hauling jobs require more than a standard truck. When you need to move crushed concrete from a demolition site, transport heavy construction equipment, or haul bulk aggregates for a road project, you need a trucking partner with the right equipment and experience. Aulo Bros provides heavy hauling services throughout the Worcester area and across Massachusetts.

Our fleet includes tri-axle dump trucks, trailer units rated for maximum legal loads, and specialized equipment for oversized materials. We've hauled everything from demolished bridge sections to industrial machinery, and we bring that experience to every job we take on.

What We Haul

Heavy hauling covers a broad range of materials and situations. In the Worcester area, we frequently handle:

  • Crushed and broken concrete from demolition projects
  • Asphalt millings and reclaimed pavement materials
  • Brick, block, and masonry debris
  • Heavy construction equipment and machinery
  • Aggregate materials including sand, gravel, and stone
  • Scrap metal and industrial byproducts

Many of these materials can be recycled or repurposed rather than landfilled. We work with regional processing facilities to recover value from demolition debris and keep usable materials in the supply chain. For a complete overview of our capabilities, visit our services page.

Local Knowledge, Regional Reach

Heavy hauling in Massachusetts means navigating weight restrictions, bridge ratings, and local permitting requirements. Our drivers know which routes work for loaded trucks and which roads to avoid. We handle the logistics so you can focus on your project.

From our Shrewsbury base, we serve Worcester and the surrounding communities efficiently. We also take on longer hauls across Massachusetts and into neighboring New England states when projects require it. Whether you need a single load moved or ongoing hauling support for a multi-month project, we scale our service to match your needs.

Equipment Built for the Job

Heavy hauling demands heavy-duty equipment maintained to high standards. Our trucks and trailers undergo regular inspection and preventive maintenance. When your project timeline depends on reliable transportation, you can count on our equipment to perform.

We also carry appropriate insurance coverage for heavy hauling operations. Before we take on a job, we verify that our coverage meets the requirements of the site and any general contractors involved.
